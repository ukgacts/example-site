Super Style Deluxe

AGENT: SUPER AGENT
 
'Keep doing what's good!'

FULL BIO

The SuperStyleDeluxe concept emerged where a young budding DJ rising through the ranks in the West Midlands making mixtapes and peddling them where he held residency at the Legendary Stump Juice night club in Wolverhampton. Titled “SuperStyle Deluxe” The name stuck!


Music production soon started, becoming signed to Hamburgs Lounge Records , Producing a succession of singles and popular remixes on BMG & Sony for the likes Bent, Pink, Dido to name but a few. To having number one chartings on releases and receiving various music nominations awards. Releasing on the Supercharged and Against the Grain labels in the Early 2000’s


The Payback Project was soon born, a pioneering label under the SuperStyle Deluxe brand, emerged and swiftly gained recognition as one of the most respected labels in its field. With a relentless pursuit of innovation and a commitment to pushing boundaries, The Payback Project helped strengthen the scene it was involved in.


SuperStyleDeluxe has decent DJ skills too! With weekly adventures up and down the UK club circuit as well as journeying on regular world tours to Australia, New Zealand, Asia, North America, Russia & Europe and was also on support duty for Pendluam. Renowned for the ability to incorporate the art of Turntabilism & connecting the dots into mixing into uptempo Bass driven DJ sets, With the ability to scratch, saw the involvement in development of the Ecler range mixers, NUO and HAK scratch mixers and was part of the Ecler Pro Team.

FULL BIO

Nixon’s been mixing since before he 1irst stood on a snowboard over 20 years ago. Being a pro-rider on the European snowboard scene for over 15 years opened up a huge freesport audience to his mixing talents and he’s been a regular at massive surf & snowboard events for years.


A random meeting with Justin Rushmore of FLR whilst snowboarding led to a move to London in 06 and to a new addiction – producing. His 2007 collaborations with D&B golden boy, Shimon, took the scene by surprise, with clubbers loving the heavily in1luenced D & B breaks - a fresh vibe in an increasingly jaded breaks scene. With three releases under the FLR wing, ‘I Like You’ received rave reviews, receiving regular airplay on both Annie Nightingale BBC and Kiss FM’s Jay Cunnings show. The second release “White Noise” went to No 13 in the DMC Buzz Charts, and ‘Around the Edge’, charged to No 1 in the Beatport Breaks chart.


Whilst Nixon’s background was rooted firmly in thrash and drum & bass, his DJ style today, is a blend of tight break necking beats, topped off with deep gut rumbling bass, enough to rock any party!


Moving forward, Nixon has stretched out his knowledge of quality music, composing music for UK computer games giant Codemasters and their Dirt 3 & 4 games. Top brands such as Lexus, Ford, CASIO, QUIKSILVER, MONSTER, NIKE, Supreme have endorsed and used Nixon beats & sound design for their Global Marketing Campaigns..

From his roots in the Freesport scene, Nixon Toured with the Tony Hawk Show as the of1icial DJ, from the UK through to Mumbai India. Also it is worth noting Nixon has produced live mixes for the mighty “The Prodigy – The Day Is My Enemy UK and European tour 2015 , No Tourist Tour in 2018 & the current Army Of The Ants European Tour 2023.

After a string of massive hits & remixes on the Stanton Warriors Punks label , Nixon has forged an original sounding and solid path through todays music scene. His signature sound could be described as a modern take on the 90/00’s classic D&B scene. 130bpm classic breaks with basslines to make you shudder!! Now he’s on Board with The Payback Project may the Nixon saga long continue!
DJ MIX:

CRUCIAL TODD

AGENT: SUPER AGENT
 
'Positive connotations only'

FULL BIO

Crucial Todd, a mysterious figure hailing from the South Coast of the UK, has been a prominent figure in the multi-genre dance music scene for an impressive three decades. However, in a bold move to shed his ego and prioritize the messages within his music, he has recently rebranded himself under an enigmatic new name. With a deep desire to elevate the awareness and spirit of his audience, Crucial Todd crafts his unique brand of music - blending Jackin House, UKG and DnB, which he aptly refers to as Nu Jackin Bass.

Exclusively signed to the esteemed Payback Project label, Crucial Todd already has an impressive array of releases under his belt and lined up for the months ahead. Already getting support from BBC Introducing and Kiss FM , his music, characterized by its quirky and infectious nature, is infused with positive messages that aim to inspire and uplift listeners. With an unwavering commitment to his craft, Crucial Todd is set to captivate audiences with his infectious beats and thought-provoking lyrics, leaving an indelible mark on the dance music scene.
